**Card** — the base surface; soft 20px radius with a whisper shadow (white) or a hairline (tinted tones).

```jsx
<Card tone="surface"><h3>When they hit or throw</h3></Card>
<Card tone="repair" onClick={open}>After the storm</Card>
```

Tones: `surface` · `well` · `accent` · `repair` · `deep` (the breather green, light text). Never stack a heavy shadow and a heavy border.
